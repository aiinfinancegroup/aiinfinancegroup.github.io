AI in Finance Group @ Makerere University, Kampala (Uganda)

Our research focuses on using Artificial Intelligence to build solutions for Money Laundering, Financial Fraud Detection and Financial Inclusion. We work with a number of stakeholder in the financial industry in Sub-Saharan Africa and our solutions are mainly for challenges in the financial sector in the African context.

Projects

Generating Synthetic Datasets for Mobile Money Transactions for AI/ML Research
This project develops a multi-agent-based simulation platform tailored for mobile money transactions from Sub-Saharan Africa. The simulation platform represents uniqiue scenarions and behaviours of mobile money actors and it is from the the interractions of the agents in the simulations that generate synthetic financial datasets that are used for AI/ML research in the domain.
