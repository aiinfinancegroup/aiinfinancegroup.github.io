AI for Finance Group @ Makerere University, Kampala (Uganda)

Our research focuses on applying Artificial Intelligence in the financial domain. Specifically, we aim to build solutions for money laundering, financial fraud detection and financial inclusion in Sub-Saharan context.

Projects

Generating Synthetic Datasets for Mobile Money Transactions for AI/ML Research
This project develops a multi-agent-based simulation platform tailored for mobile money transactions from Sub-Saharan Africa. The simulation platform represents uniqiue scenarios and behaviours of mobile money actors and the interractions of the agents in the simulation platform generates synthetic financial datasets that are used for AI/ML research in the domain.
